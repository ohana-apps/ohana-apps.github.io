About

Ohana Labs is a one-person indie studio based in Israel. We design and build mobile apps and games for iOS and Android, with a bias toward simple ideas executed with patience. Software you can hand to a kid, a parent, or a friend — and feel good about.

What we make

Casual games

Pick-up-and-play experiences that respect your time. No grind, no manipulation — just a few good minutes.

Everyday tools

Small utilities that solve a specific, real problem cleanly. The kind of app you keep on the home screen.

Crafted to last

We polish before we ship and maintain after we ship. Built for the long quiet life on your device.

How we work

  1. 01 Privacy by default. Minimum data, no tracking SDKs, no behavioral ads. If we don’t need it, we don’t collect it.
  2. 02 Localized from day one. English and Hebrew shipped together, RTL done properly. More languages as we grow.
  3. 03 Slow software. Fewer features, better ones. We’d rather ship one good thing than ten loud ones.

What’s next
