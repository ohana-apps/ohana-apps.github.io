Tap It – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tap It ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the Game, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

The Game is designed for players of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the Game experience.

Information you provide:

  • Username (chosen by you)

Information generated automatically:

  • Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase Authentication)
  • Tap count / game score
  • Speed Tap scores and attempt history
  • Friend code (randomly generated)
  • Recovery code (randomly generated)
  • Friend connections (friend codes you add)
  • Last active timestamp

Information collected by Firebase infrastructure:

Google Firebase (our backend provider) may automatically collect certain technical data as part of its standard operations, including IP addresses and basic device/network information, for security and service reliability purposes. This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy.

We do NOT collect: email addresses, real names, phone numbers, location data, or photos/media.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the Game functionality
  • Display your score on leaderboards
  • Enable the friend system
  • Allow account recovery on new devices
  • Sync your game progress across sessions

We do not use your information for profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the Game.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), which are hosted on Google Cloud Platform. Data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL).

Your tap count is also stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences for offline play.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your username and score are visible to other players through the leaderboard feature. Your friend code may be shared by you with others to enable the friend system.

We may share data with:

  • Google Firebase (as our data processor/infrastructure provider)
  • Third-party ad networks that display advertisements within the Game (such as Google AdMob or similar services). These networks may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data as described in their own privacy policies.
  • Law enforcement if required by law

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

The Game is designed for players of all ages, including children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is described in this policy
  • We do not require email or real name registration
  • The only user-provided information is a chosen username
  • We do not engage in behavioral advertising targeted at children
  • Leaderboard participation only displays the chosen username

Parents or guardians: If you believe your child has provided information beyond what is described here, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's use of the Game and to help them choose an appropriate username that does not reveal personal information.

7. Advertising

The Game displays, or may at any time begin displaying, advertisements from third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob or similar services). By using the Game, you consent to the presence of advertisements, which may be introduced or modified at any time without additional notice.

Information that ad networks may collect:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For users under 13:

  • We will use commercially reasonable efforts to serve age-appropriate advertisements
  • We will not knowingly serve personalized or behaviorally targeted ads to children
  • We will configure ad networks to comply with COPPA where supported

For all users:

  • You may be able to limit ad tracking through your device settings
  • Third-party ad networks operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (visible in-app through your profile)
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us
  • Stop using the Game at any time by uninstalling it

If you uninstall the Game, your local data is automatically deleted. To request deletion of your server-side data, please contact us.

9. Administrative Access

Authorized personnel may access or modify game data (such as your tap count) for legitimate administrative purposes, including correcting errors or investigating abuse. Such access is limited to what is necessary and is not used for any purpose unrelated to operating the Game.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account exists and the Game is operational. If you wish to have your data deleted, please contact us.

Inactive accounts (no activity for 12 months or more) may be deleted at our discretion.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the United States (where Google Cloud servers may be located). By using the Game, you consent to this transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will indicate the date of the latest revision at the top of this page. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or if you wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us at: ohana.ai.apps@gmail.com
