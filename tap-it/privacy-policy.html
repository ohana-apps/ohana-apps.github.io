Tap It – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tap It ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the Game, you consent to the practices described in this policy. The Game is designed for players of all ages, including children. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the Game experience:

Information you provide:

  • Username (chosen by you)

Information generated automatically:

  • Anonymous user ID (generated by Firebase Authentication)
  • Tap count / game score
  • Friend code (randomly generated)
  • Recovery code (randomly generated)
  • Friend connections (friend codes you add)
  • Last active timestamp

We do NOT collect: email addresses, real names, phone numbers, location data, device identifiers for tracking, or photos/media.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to provide and maintain Game functionality, display scores on leaderboards, enable the friend system, allow account recovery, and sync game progress. We do not use your information for profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose unrelated to the Game.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services (Firestore and Firebase Authentication), hosted on Google Cloud Platform, transmitted using TLS/SSL. Tap count is also stored locally using SharedPreferences for offline play.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. Your username and score are visible to other players via leaderboards. We may share data with Google Firebase (infrastructure provider), third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob), and law enforcement if required by law.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 beyond what is described here. No email or real name is required. The only user-provided information is a chosen username. We do not engage in behavioral advertising targeted at children. Parents: if you believe your child has provided information beyond what is described here, contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Advertising

The Game displays, or may at any time begin displaying, advertisements from third-party ad networks (such as Google AdMob). Ad networks may collect device identifiers, IP address, device info, and ad interaction data. For users under 13, we will use commercially reasonable efforts to serve age-appropriate, non-personalized ads.

8. Your Rights

You may access your data in-app, request deletion by contacting us, or stop using the Game at any time by uninstalling it.

9. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your account exists. Inactive accounts (12+ months) may be deleted at our discretion.

10. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers outside your country of residence, including in the United States. By using the Game, you consent to this transfer.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance.
