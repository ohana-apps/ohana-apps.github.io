Afterglow - Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Afterglow ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Game.

The current version of Afterglow is an offline puzzle game. It does not use accounts, cloud save, third-party advertising, analytics, crash reporting, in-app purchases, or a server backend.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The Game stores gameplay and settings data locally on your device so the Game can function properly. This may include:

  • Completed levels, stars, moves, phases, and completion timestamps
  • Lumens, hints, unlocked skins, and selected skin
  • Sound and haptic settings
  • Onboarding status, accepted terms status, and current chapter

This information is stored locally using platform storage. We do not currently receive, upload, or sync this gameplay data to a server.

3. No Account or Cloud Backup

The current version of the Game does not include user accounts, cloud save, or cross-device sync. If you uninstall the Game, clear app data, reset your device, or move to a new device, your progress may be permanently lost. We cannot recover progress that exists only on your device.

4. Information You Send to Us

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, message, screenshots, device details, or support request details. We use this information only to respond to you, provide support, protect the Game, and keep appropriate records.

5. Information We Do Not Currently Collect

The current version of the Game does not knowingly collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or account login
  • Precise location
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or media files
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics events, crash reports, or server-side gameplay data

6. Advertising, Analytics, Purchases, and Online Features

The current version of the Game does not display third-party ads, use analytics, use crash reporting, offer in-app purchases, or connect to online services.

We may add features in the future, such as ads, rewarded ads, analytics, crash reporting, purchases, accounts, leaderboards, cloud save, or other online services. If we add these features, they may involve third-party service providers and may collect information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, diagnostics, gameplay events, purchase information, account identifiers, IP address, approximate location, or ad interaction data, depending on the feature.

Before enabling features that materially change our data practices, we will update this Privacy Policy and the relevant app store disclosures. Where required by law or platform rules, we will request consent or provide choices before collecting or using data for those features.

7. Children's Privacy

The Game is designed as a general-audience puzzle game and does not require users to provide personal information. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the Game. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has sent us personal information, please contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal information. Because the current version does not upload gameplay data to our servers, we do not share gameplay progress with third parties.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, to respond to legal requests, or to investigate misuse of the Game.

9. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you delete it, reset progress inside the Game, clear app data, or uninstall the Game. Email support messages may be retained for as long as needed to handle your request, maintain records, and protect our legal interests.

10. Your Choices

You can stop all local data collection by uninstalling the Game. You can also reset your local progress from the Game settings. If you contacted us by email and want us to delete support messages associated with your request, contact us at the email below.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when the policy was most recently changed. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
